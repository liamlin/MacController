Cntrl.io

This service helps you control your MacOS system volume and the Firefox tabs with you smartphone, via the Internet.

Step 1: Download Mac Controller Helper if you want to control your Mac Step 2: Get Firefox! Step 3: Download and install the Firefox add-on Step 4: Click on the add-on icon and scan the QR code with your smartphone. Final Step: Open the link with a modern mobile browser and have fun playing with it.
